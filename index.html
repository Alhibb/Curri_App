Frequently Asked Questions

What age group is this program suitable for?

Our program is designed for children aged 7-15 years. We have different levels to accommodate various age groups and prior experience.

Does my child need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is needed! Our curriculum starts from the basics and progressively builds up to more advanced concepts.

What equipment will my child need?

We provide all necessary equipment during classes. For home practice, a basic computer or tablet with internet access is sufficient.

How long is the program?

The core program runs for 12 weeks with classes twice a week. We also offer extended programs and holiday camps.

Are there any payment plans available?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. You can pay in installments or take advantage of our early bird discounts.
